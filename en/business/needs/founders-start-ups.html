Company founders and startups

Hit the ground running

You need more than one good idea to take successful steps towards independence. It requires business acumen, perseverance, expertise, a good network and professional support. Together with various partners, PostFinance supports emerging startups by offering attractive services, valuable expertise and helpful tools.

Information

Do you want to bring your business idea to life? If you are founding your own company in Switzerland, there are a number of business requirements. We have put together some useful documents and tips on founding a company so you are ideally prepared for this step.

Founding a company

For simple financial transactions PostFinance offers an attractive startup package, which is tailored perfectly to startup needs. You benefit from free account management with e-finance during the first two years. There are also other suitable offers that will help you make a successful start in the world of business.
