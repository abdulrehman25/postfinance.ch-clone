All you need for your startup

Our startup package for your own business
Order now

Whatever your business ideas are, PostFinance will support you with the right products and expertise.

Startup package

We will waive your account management fees with e-finance for two years.

  • Order the startup package

  • The startup package with e-finance is available as a business account in either CHF or EUR. Account management is free of charge for the first two years.

    • Business account in CHF
    • Business account in EUR
    • Online banking in e-finance

  • For payments as an entrepreneur

    PostFinance Visa Business Card credit card with cashback on sales turnover.

    Your advantage: the first credit card free of charge for 1 year.

    • More information on the PostFinance Visa Business Card

    For your online shop revenue

    PostFinance Checkout (All-In-One or Flex) as a payment solution for your online shop. It will enable you to process all standard payment methods

    Your advantage: If you sign up for a paid software licence (All-In-One or Flex), you’ll receive 6 months free of charge.

    • More information on PostFinance Checkout All-In-One
    • More information on PostFinance Checkout Flex

    Read more at postfinance.ch/startups

    More information on founding a company.

    • Find out more at postfinance.ch/startups

  • For payments as an entrepreneur

    PostFinance Card: a versatile card for your business account – for making payments in stores, online and for cash withdrawals worldwide.

    • More information on the PostFinance Card

    For your in-store revenue

    EFT/POS at salespoints so your customers can make cashless in-store payments (payment terminal).

    • More information on EFT/POS

    For your startup capital as a limited liability company or private limited company

    Capital payment account: so you can securely raise your startup capital as you found your limited liability company or private limited company, and handle ordinary capital increases or subsequent payments of capital.

    • More information on the capital payment account

    • Your company was founded within the past two years
    • Company domicile/company headquarters in Switzerland
    • No special conditions will be applied retroactively to products that have already been purchased
