Public-sector entities

The right solutions

Whether to process payment transactions efficiently or to bridge liquidity shortfalls: PostFinance offers public-sector entities tailored solutions with favourable conditions and professional advice on site.

Account & e-finance

With the business account from PostFinance, you have access to a wide range of services in the fields of payment transactions, investments or financing at any time via e-finance, allowing you to take care of your day-to-day financial transactions efficiently and cost-effectively.
