Banking package

Simply smart – our banking packages

The appropriate banking package for you as of 1 July 2021: our banking packages bundle accounts and services together into a fair, transparent range of services, and are tailored to your needs. E-finance and the PostFinance App allow you to carry out your banking transactions securely and conveniently online.

Banking packages for adults

Smart

CHF 5/month
  • Private account in CHF, EUR or foreign currency
  • PostFinance Card + free cash withdrawal at Postomats
  • E-finance + PostFinance App with security guarantee
  • Savings account in CHF and EUR
  • Cashless payment with TWINT

Additional fee-based services

  • Credit cards from CHF 50/year
  • Cash withdrawals from third-party ATMs: CHF 2/withdrawal
  • Cash withdrawals abroad: CHF 5/withdrawal

SmartPlus

CHF 12/month
  • Private account in CHF, EUR or foreign currency
  • PostFinance Card + free cash withdrawals at Postomats
  • E-finance + PostFinance App with security guarantee
  • Savings account in CHF and EUR
  • Cashless payments with TWINT
Including
  • Classic/Standard or
  • Mastercard Value (prepaid) credit card or
  • CHF 50 discount on Gold or Platinum

Free cash withdrawals at all third-party ATMs in Switzerland and abroad with the PostFinance Card.

Young adults between 21 and 30 years of age who are starting out in their professional life after completing their education, receive the SmartPlus banking package for a maximum of three years for half the price at CHF 6.

The price for a banking package and for the paper option applies per customer relationship. A customer relationship can include several accounts. The fees are charged monthly.

Banking packages for children, young people, apprentices and students

SmartKids

0 to 11 years, free of charge
  • Youth account in CHF or EUR
  • Youth savings account in CHF and EUR
  • On request with e-finance, PostFinance App and PostFinance Card for the parents

SmartYoung

12 to 20 years, free of charge
  • Youth account in CHF or EUR
  • Youth savings account in CHF and EUR
  • PostFinance Card
  • E-finance and PostFinance App with security guarantee
  • Cashless payments with TWINT

Including

Mastercard Value (prepaid) credit card from the age of 12 or Classic/Standard credit card from the age of 18.

Free cash withdrawals at third-party ATMs in Switzerland and abroad with the PostFinance Card.

SmartStudents

18 to 30 years, free of charge
  • Student account in CHF or EUR
  • Savings account in CHF and EUR
  • PostFinance Card
  • E-finance and PostFinance App with security guarantee
  • Cashless payments with TWINT

Including

Mastercard Value (prepaid) or Classic/Standard credit card.

Free cash withdrawals at third-party ATMs in Switzerland and abroad with the PostFinance Card.

The price for a banking package and for the paper option applies per customer relationship. A customer relationship can include several accounts. The fees are charged monthly.

How do you get your banking package?

The new banking packages will be launched on 1 July 2021. The price for your banking package and, where applicable, the paper option, applies per customer relationship and for all accounts in the same customer relationship.

You have a PostFinance account

If you already have one or more accounts with PostFinance, you will automatically receive, as of 1 July 2021, the banking package that is most in line with your use of our services. You will be informed about this by letter and in e-finance. You can change your banking package in e-finance, or by using the reply form, or by telephone.

E-finance login

You do not have a PostFinance account

Open the services of your choice. As of 1 July 2021, you will automatically receive the banking package that is most in line with how you use our services. You will be informed about this by letter and in e-finance. You can change your banking package in e-finance, or by using the reply form, or by telephone.

    • You can easily cancel the paper option in e-finance under: “Settings and profile”, “My products”, “Fees”. If you do not have e-finance yet, you can order it at any time. You will then receive all notifications digitally in e-finance.

      Example: The customer is charged the annual price of CHF 50 for a Classic/Standard or Value (prepaid) credit card in January 2021 (card price for the period from January to December 2021). However, because the SmartPlus package price already includes the Classic/Standard or Value (prepaid) credit card as of 1 July 2021, the customer receives back 6/12 of the CHF 50 paid in January 2021 for the period from July to December 2021, i.e. CHF 25 in this example. The refund is made to the private account in August 2021.

Please note that for customers residing abroad, the CHF 25 fee per payment transaction account remains unchanged regardless of your choice of banking package.
