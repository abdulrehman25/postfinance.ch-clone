Your mortgage

Personalized advice

Protect yourself and benefit from attractive interest rates. Benefit from low interest rates from 0.95% if you take out a new 10-year fixed-rate mortgage or switch from an existing third-party mortgage to one provided by PostFinance.

Would you like to get an overview?

Find the right type of financing for your property and secure our attractive interest rates for your preferred term. Calculate how much your future dream home will cost and request a non-binding personal mortgage offer online.

Would you like to purchase, build or renovate a property?

Use the “Financing your own home – quick check” to calculate whether your preferred financing satisfies the affordability and lending guidelines. We would be happy to offer you a personal consultation and show you which mortgages and terms are suitable for you.

Arrange an appointment

Would you like to switch an existing mortgage to PostFinance?

We offer attractive interest rates and handle all the admin for you. We will also cover up to 500 francs in transfer costs if you are charged fees for switching. Complete your mortgage request directly online and benefit from our online mortgages’ attractive interest rates. We can also put together a personal quotation for you during a consultation.
