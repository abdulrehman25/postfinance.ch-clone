PostFinance TWINT

Switzerland’s digital cash

Are you twinting yet? One app – several functions Pay online quickly, shop conveniently, split the bill easily: with TWINT, Switzerland’s digital cash.

PostFinance TWINT: more than just making payments

  • Link the PostFinance TWINT App with your account

  • Pay quickly and conveniently in the online shop, at the checkout, at machines and at parking meters

  • Request money or send cash to friends and relatives in real time – simply, from smartphone to smartphone

  • Activate your loyalty cards and enjoy the benefits

  • Store customer cards and membership cards and collect points with every payment you make

  • Twint+ is the secure way to make donations via the app, to pay at the parking meter and to buy digital vouchers for yourself or as a gift

    • In order to use PostFinance TWINT, you require a private account with PostFinance
    • To activate PostFinance TWINT, you will need a PostFinance Card with a private account and a yellow card reader, or the PostFinance App with a registered PostFinance login

    • Download the PostFinance TWINT App directly from Google Play or the App Store
    • One-off registration via the PostFinance App and PostFinance login or with the PostFinance Card and the yellow card reader
    • After registration, you can store your preferred method of payment as well as customer cards and membership cards

    Tip: activate fingerprint identification or facial recognition  for easy use of the app.

    You can store an increasing number of cards digitally in the TWINT App. Be it a customer card like the Coop Supercard or a membership card: each time you pay with TWINT, you can enjoy the benefits of whatever card you’ve stored.

    Loyalty cards

    Get discounts by activating the loyalty cards available in your TWINT App and collecting all the electronic stamps you need.

    Coupons

    When twinting, benefit directly from discount coupons, which you can find and activate in your TWINT App.
