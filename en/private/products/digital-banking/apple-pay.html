Apple Pay

Your PostFinance credit card with Apple Pay
Order credit cards now

Apple Pay is the easy way to pay quickly with your PostFinance prepaid card or credit card on an iPhone or another Apple device. Apple Pay is contactless, safe and easy to set up.

Add your credit card to Apple Pay

It’s easy for you to add your PostFinance prepaid card or credit card to Apple Pay. Here’s how it works:

  • On an iPhone: Open the Wallet app, tap the “+” sign in the top right and then follow the instructions.
  • On an Apple Watch: Open the Apple Watch app on your iPhone, tap “Wallet & Apple Pay” and then “Add Card”.
  • Instruction video on apple.com The link will open in a new window

Pay quickly and safely with Apple Pay

Apple Pay makes it easier and safer to pay in shops, apps and online. You can make purchases quickly with the Apple devices you use every day.

  • Instruction video for Face ID on apple.com The link will open in a new window
  • Instruction video for Touch ID on apple.com The link will open in a new window

Where you can pay using Apple Pay

Use Apple Pay wherever you see one of these symbols

    • A list of The link will open in a new window devices compatible with Apple Pay
    • Apple Wallet app
    • PostFinance credit card or prepaid card

  • Set up Apple Pay

    • iPhone: Open the Wallet app, tap the “+” sign in the top right and then follow the instructions.
    • Apple Watch: Open the Apple Watch app on your iPhone, tap “Wallet & Apple Pay” and then “Add Card”.
    • iPad: Open “Wallet & Apple Pay” in the settings and select “Add Card”
    • MacBook Pro with Touch ID: Select “Wallet & Apple Pay” in System Preferences, then click on “Add Card”.

    Pay using Apple Pay

    • Pay using Face ID: Double-click the side button on your iPhone, glance at your screen, then hold it near the reader.
    • Pay using Touch ID: Hold your iPhone near the reader with your finger on the Touch ID sensor.

    When you make a purchase, Apple Pay uses a device-specific number along with a unique transaction code. Your full card number is never stored on your device or on Apple servers and cannot be shared with retailers. To ensure all your purchases are confidential, Apple Pay does not store any transaction data that can be traced back to you.
