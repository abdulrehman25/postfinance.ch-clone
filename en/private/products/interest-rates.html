Current interest rates

  • Date of last interest rate change: 1 December 2019

    For private and savings accounts in Swiss francs and euros with assets of at least 250,000 Swiss francs (or the equivalent in euros), PostFinance basically charges a customer asset fee of 0.75 percent on the credit balance above this amount.

    In addition, for customers with substantial assets on their private and savings accounts who do not use any other products, PostFinance charges a customer asset fee of 0.75 percent for assets from 100,000 Swiss francs (or the equivalent in euros).

    The affected customers will be informed in writing of all potential changes in good time. They can reduce or avoid the customer asset fee by investing part of their assets in funds or other securities.

    The customer asset fee is calculated per customer relationship for all private and savings accounts in francs and euros and is debited monthly.
