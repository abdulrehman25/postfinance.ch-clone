PostFinance Visa Gold Card and PostFinance Mastercard Gold

Get money back on all purchases worldwide
Order now

With the PostFinance Visa Gold and PostFinance Mastercard® Gold credit cards, you can shop online or in-store around the world, withdraw cash and make contactless payments at the checkout.

PostFinance Visa Gold Card and PostFinance Mastercard Gold: get money back on all purchases worldwide

  • Make purchases and withdraw cash worldwide

  • Pay with Apple Pay and Samsung Pay

  • Bonus programme – with up to 1% cashback

  • Management 24/7 in e-finance and via the PostFinance App

  • Partial payment possible

  • With the unlimited cash bonus programme for the Gold cards from PostFinance, you can benefit from a turnover reimbursement of 0.5%. In the first year you even receive a double bonus. The payment is made directly into your private account every six months, in June and December.

    Cashback on the sales turnover achieved:

    • 1,0% in thefirst year
    • 0,5% from the second year

  • Design

    24/7 management in e-finance and via the PostFinance App

    Thanks to secure e-finance, you have your credit card under control at all times:

    • Real-time display of all your transactions, card limits and available amounts
    • Load credit onto your card to increase your available amounts
    • Receive free notifications about completed transactions (push or text notifications)
    • Block or unblock your card and order a replacement card or a replacement PIN
    • Set the preferred payment method for the monthly credit card invoice

    Mobile payment solutions

    Pay securely, more easily and quickly. The PostFinance Visa Gold Card and Mastercard Gold can be stored with the following providers:

    • Apple Pay
    • Samsung Pay
    • Swatch Pay
    • TWINT

    AVIS – discounted car rental

    Book your rented vehicle via the link below and pay with your PostFinance Visa Gold Card or PostFinance Mastercard Gold. You will receive the discount automatically.

    • Go to www.avis.ch

    Rental vehicles can be booked by calling 0848 811 818. Quote the AWD number (Avis Worldwide Discount number) during the call. The number can be found on the back of your Gold card.

    Card security

    Make secure payments worldwide with PostFinance credit cards thanks to the latest chip technology and 3-D Secure.

    • 3-D Secure – shop online easily and securely
    • Make contactless payments with the card
    • More information about card security

  • Preconditions

    The applicant

    • resides in Switzerland or Liechtenstein (country of domicile),
    • is at least 18 years old
    • and has a private account in CHF from PostFinance
    • has gross annual income of CHF 75,000

    Card prices

    From 1 July our accounts and services will be bundled together in banking packages. With the SmartPlus banking package, you will receive a reduction of CHF 50 on a credit card.

    • Prices and conditions from 1 July 2021
    Main card
    CHF 90 per year
    Partner card/second card
    CHF 40 per year

    Replacement cards

    Via hotline/customer advisor
    CHF 30 per card
    Via e-finance
    CHF 25 per card

    Payments and cash withdrawals

    Monthly spending limit
    Individual
    Transactions abroad (CHF/foreign currencies)
    1.2% processing surcharge (The exchange rate (selling rate) used is set once a day and is valid for processing on the next working day.)
    Cash withdrawals at ATMs in Switzerland and abroad
    3.5% commission (minimum CHF 10)
    Monthly cash withdrawal limit
    • CHF 1,000 per day from ATMs
    • Max. 40% of spending limit

    Payment methods

    Payment period for monthly bill
    20 days, default interest charged thereafter
    Part payment
    • Possible, if there is no risk of overindebtedness
    • 9.5% effective annual interest rate
