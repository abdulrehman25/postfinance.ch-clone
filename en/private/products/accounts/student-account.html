Student account

The ideal account for apprentices and students
Open now

Starting a career is an exciting time. The free PostFinance student account enables you to create the ideal foundation for your financial future. Thanks to e-finance and the PostFinance App, you can keep an eye on your daily financial transactions at all times – leaving you more time for self-fulfilment.

Student account: for customers aged 18 to 30 who are in education or training

  • Ideal as a salary account for apprentices and students

  • Preferential interest rate

  • Free account management

  • Free PostFinance Card

  • On request: free Mastercard Standard or Visa Classic Card

    • Student account in CHF or EUR
    • Account management: either online (e-finance) or on paper
    • Monthly account statement and annual account balancing with interest statement
    • Lower issuing commission for PostFinance Fonds
    • Free Mastercard Standard or Visa Classic Card

      PostFinance Ltd

    • Transfer form (PDF) The link will open in a new window

    How can I convert my youth account or private account into a student account?

    If you meet the preconditions for a student account, please send us a copy of your training contract or proof of education via e-finance or by post. Your youth account or private account will be converted into a student account after your details have been checked.

    • postfinance.ch/proof-of-education

    • Services and prices for private customers The link will open in a new window

  • Where can you withdraw cash at Migros and Manor?

    Cash withdrawals are possible at the Customer Service checkout.

    What are the preconditions for a student account?

    • You are between 18 and 30 years of age
    • You are in education or training for a period of at least one year, full-time or while working
    • Education or training includes: vocational training, middle school, technical education, higher technical education, university of applied sciences and university

    What is the overdraft limit for a student account?

    The account must not be overdrawn. If creditworthiness checks are positive and regular credits are made, PostFinance may grant an overdraft limit of CHF 1,000 francs and change it at any time on request.
