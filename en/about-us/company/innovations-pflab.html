Innovations

Welcome to the PFLab innovation laboratory

Experimenting, piloting and innovating in future-related areas: PostFinance’s PFLab drives innovations, and is also developing business areas and models of the future outside of its current core business. To generate innovation, we work with start-ups, companies, universities of applied sciences and traditional universities within our development areas, and we live and breathe open innovation. Find out what our innovation partners can expect from us here. We look forward to working with you!

Sharing knowledge and learning from and with one another: by working with universities of applied sciences and traditional universities, we forge links that continually produce exciting (research) projects.

Why pursue open innovation?

Open innovation is about opening up the innovation process to the outside world. By involving external partners, we see things we would not have thought of on our own.
