Participations

PostFinance is way ahead of the game when it comes to new market trends – and it plays an active role in shaping the future. That’s why it works with various cooperation and service partners on request, and chooses in particular to participate in young, innovative companies. PostFinance is especially interested in start-ups in the fintech sector.

PostFinance always tailors its collaborations to its partners’ needs. To ensure every company can benefit as much as possible, it offers various types of participation: corporate venture capital, innovation participations and infrastructure participations. However, PostFinance is also open to other innovative types of participation.

  • Find out more about Corporate venture Capital
  • Find out more about innovation participations
  • Find out more about infrastructure and other participations

Corporate venture capital

Through its corporate venture capital (CVC) programme, PostFinance participates in innovative start-ups and supports them with limited funds in the form of equity. This also means PostFinance is directly involved in market changes, enabling it to identify emerging products, services, technologies and business models at an early stage. PostFinance is especially interested in high-growth start-ups in the fintech sector.

In terms of strategic thrusts, PostFinance is especially interested in high-growth start-ups in the fintech sector. The start-ups benefit from a reliable, efficient and long term investor.

Do you want to find out more about corporate venture capital? Contact us at venturing@postfinance.ch.
