QR generator

Generate a QR-bill quickly and easily in just a few steps. The QR-bill consists of a payment part and a receipt.

If the QR-bill is used for physical payment in a post office branch, it must be printed on white, perforated paper. You can obtain perforated paper either from the The link will open in a new window Postshop or from a print store.
