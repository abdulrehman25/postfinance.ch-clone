Association software

The software solutions for your association

With the ClubDesk or ClubLight software solutions you can accomplish all of your association’s administrative tasks in a single programme. Choose the software solution that best matches your association’s needs and benefit from attractive special conditions as a PostFinance customer.

All software packages include modules for invoicing, automatic monitoring of accounts receivable and member and customer management as standard. You can find more information about the various software solutions in the relevant sections or directly on the provider’s website. 

ClubDesk

Clubdesk is a web-based software solution available in three versions:

  • Free version for associations with up to 200 members
  • Fee-based version, ClubDesk Pro, for small and medium-sized associations
  • Fee-based version, ClubDesk XL, for larger associations
  • Compatible with the QR-bill

As a PostFinance customer, you can use the fee-based versions free of charge in the first six months by entering the promo code POSTFINANCE-CH. You will subsequently receive a 20% discount on the applicable annual fee. 

  • More information at clubdesk.com The link will open in a new window

ClubLight

ClubLight is a web-based software solution available as a paid version with a recurring annual fee. With ClubLight you can create your own lists, reports and tables to suit your needs.
As a PostFinance customer, you will receive a reduction on the set-up fee and recurring annual fees. In the first year you will save more than CHF 200 and only pay CHF 49 for each subsequent year instead of CHF 120.

  • More information at clublight.leanux.ch The link will open in a new window
