It's in your hands — the new PostFinance App

Your finances, always under control
Update the app Update the app

The new PostFinance App has arrived and is available for download from Google Play or the App Store. Those who used the previous PostFinance App only have to update it – the new app is already available on the smartphone.

Update the PostFinance AppUpdate the PostFinance App

The app that was introduced in March will be fully effective on 10 May 2021. The previous PostFinance App will be deactivated and will no longer be operational from that date. Please download the update beforehand. Simply follow the instructions in the app to ensure that you can continue to use it. Please note that you will then require an e-finance login.

  • Electronic invoices (eBill)

    Major improvements to eBill You will be able to view electronic invoices in the app once again. You can also switch to the eBill platform, for instance to subscribe to new invoice issuers. This means you will have access to the same options you have in the e-finance desktop.

    Facial recognition for Android users

    The “Face Unlock” feature is now supported on Android devices. All Android users will now be able to register for login via the app.

    Fast service

    The key features of the fast service (e.g. checking your account balance, sending money to mobile numbers, buying digital credit or reserving Benefit offers) will continue to be available in the new PostFinance App. However, you now need access to e-finance in order to use the features.

    Become a PostFinance customer

    Anyone who doesn’t yet have a customer relationship with PostFinance can open an account, sign up to e-finance and take out a credit card using the app – a completely digital, paperless process.

    International payments

    For the time being, international payments cannot be made directly from the app. However, until the function is integrated into the app, they can be made by switching from the app to e-finance via the mobile browser. You will need to log in again.

    Display and design of your accounts

    The display of your accounts and assets will be updated in the new PostFinance App. These will now be sorted by payment accounts, savings accounts, e-trading, etc. You can also use dark mode in the app.

    Technical questions

    • PostFinance offers apps for Android and iPhone. The following operating systems are supported for the use of these apps.

      Mobile operating systems

    • The new PostFinance App will be launched in stages from 3 March, and will be available for all customers to download and update from Google Play and the App Store by mid-March. 

    • Once you have updated to the new PostFinance App, you will be unable to switch back to the old version.

    • The app can be used on several devices. However, the biometric login feature (Face ID and fingerprint identification) will only work on one device. Alternatively, you can use a different login method on your other devices (e.g. Mobile ID or a card reader).

    • You can copy and paste passwords from the password manager into the app. The password manager will not enter your e-finance login details automatically for you. Alternatively, you can click on “Skip password” when you log in to the app. In future, you will be able to log in with Face ID or fingerprint identification directly.

    Functions and instructions

    • “Scan + Pay” via PostFinance App

      Using the “Scan + Pay” function, you can scan inpayment slips with reference numbers (ISR) and QR-bills directly in e-finance via the app. The invoices will be approved immediately. By default, your main account is selected as the debit account. However, you can select another debit account.

      Here’s how it works: in the e-finance section of the app, tap on “Scan + Pay”, then “Next” and “Scan”. Hold your smartphone’s camera over the code line of the inpayment slip.
