Financial Services Act (FinSA)

The Federal Act on Financial Services (FinSA) has been in force since 1 January 2020. This act is designed to protect the customers of financial service providers and to help boost the reputation and competitiveness of the Swiss financial center.

Below you will find important information on PostFinance, the ombudsman and customer segmentation under FinSA at PostFinance.

  • PostFinance Ltd is licensed as a bank under the Federal Act on Banks and Savings Banks (BankA). PostFinance is monitored by the Swiss Financial Market Supervisory Authority (FINMA).

  • FinSA sets out several different customer segments: retail customers, professional customers and institutional customers. Retail customers are fully protected by FinSA, whereas restrictions are possible in the case of professional and institutional customers. At PostFinance, all customers are treated as retail customers as defined by FinSA. As a PostFinance customer, you benefit from all the rights established in FinSA and receive maximum protection.
