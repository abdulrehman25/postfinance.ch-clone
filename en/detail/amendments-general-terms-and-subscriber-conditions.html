Amendments to General Terms and Conditions and Subscriber Conditions

More services, less small print

We are continuously developing and optimizing our products and services. The same applies to our General Terms and Conditions (GTC) and Subscriber Conditions (SC), which we have simplified and brought in line with the digital reality.

What does that mean for you?

In addition to the GTC, there are now three SC which govern the fundamental aspects of the business relationship between you and PostFinance.

The new conditions apply as of 1 September 2020. If you agree then you do not need to take any further action. If you do not agree to our changes, you may terminate your business relationship with PostFinance.
