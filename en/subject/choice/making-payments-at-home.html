How to pay invoices in e-finance

Making transfers and entering invoices with the payment assistant

Our payment assistant guides you step by step through the process of making payments on your PC. Payment entry and management takes just a few seconds.

  • Continue to payment assistant

Pay bills with Scan + Pay – scan and approve inpayment slips

Scan and directly approve inpayment slips in e-finance via the PostFinance App on your smartphone. This saves you the time-consuming task of entering the account and reference numbers.

  • Continue to Scan + Pay

Pay eBill invoices in just a few clicks

eBill allows you to receive invoices from companies and suppliers electronically on request. After checking them, you can approve them for payment or reject or modify them directly in e-finance. This saves you the tedious task of typing in reference numbers.

  • Continue to eBill

Pay your invoices with a payment order

You can request payment order forms with ease in writing or by phone. Send the payment documents by mail along with the total amount, execution date and signed payment order.

  • Ordering a payment order
  • General information on payment orders

How to log in to e-finance

Login via PostFinance App – with your fingerprint or Face ID

The easiest and most convenient way to log in to e-finance is via your smartphone: after a one-time activation in the PostFinance App, all that’s needed is your fingerprint or FaceID.

  • Continue to login via app
  • Alternative login options

Have you forgotten or lost your login data?

If you no longer have your security elements, such as e-finance number / username or password, we would be happy to help you. Please phone 0848 888 710 (max. CHF 0.08/min. in Switzerland).
