Security

Now you can play it safe with digital banking
Ordering e-finance

Security is a top priority for PostFinance. If something were to happen, PostFinance will assume responsibility for any damage. But it’s also important that the customer also takes steps to guarantee a secure digital banking experience. We’ll show you how in our free courses.

Damage cover

Incorrect data entry may lead to damage. If a Trojan or computer virus manages to get through and cause damage, PostFinance will provide cover for up to CHF 100,000. However, you need to report the damage to us without delay and observe the liability conditions.

Security standards for those who want a secure experience

Our security standards are aimed at those who want to use e-finance and the Internet securely, whether at home or on the go.

  • E-finance and Internet

    The multi-level security system

    Security is PostFinance’s utmost priority: your payments are protected by a multi-level firewall, encryption and our automatic transaction monitoring.

    • To the security tips

  • SecureBrowser

    A secure connection at all times

    Protect yourself against Trojans, phishing attacks and data theft. SecureBrowser works like any other browser but does not require installation, administrator rights or access to your operating system.

    • To the SecureBrowser

  • Browsercheck

    Keep your protection up to date

    Protect yourself against online dangers by ensuring your system is always up to date. The browser check scans your computer for outdated software and helps you resolve any vulnerabilities found.

    • To the Browsercheck

  • Phishing and Malware

    Recognize attacks

    Phishing

    Here, the fraudsters are attempting to access Internet users’ confidential data with the help of fake e-mails.

    Malware

    A collective term for programs that cause damage. This includes viruses, for example.
